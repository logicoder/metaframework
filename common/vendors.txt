VENDORS
----------------------------------------

For optimization and ease of updates the common/vendors folder isn't included, so create it and
download a copy, link from a common dir or set as externals the following libraries:

Browscap http://browsers.garykeith.com/downloads.asp (only php_browscap.ini is needed)
CFPropertyList https://github.com/rodneyrehm/CFPropertyList
PHPass http://www.openwall.com/phpass/ (only PasswordHash.php is needed)
PHPExcel https://phpexcel.svn.codeplex.com/svn/branches/v1.7.6/Classes
php-helpers https://github.com/logicoder/php-helpers
PHPMailer https://phpmailer.svn.sourceforge.net/svnroot/phpmailer/phpmailer/tags/phpmailer-5.1
Upload http://www.verot.net/php_class_upload_download.htm
Yii http://yii.googlecode.com/svn/tags/1.1.8/framework OR http://yii.googlecode.com/svn/trunk/framework
Zend http://framework.zend.com/svn/framework/standard/tags/release-1.11.10/library/Zend
